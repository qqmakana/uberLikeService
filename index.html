About Our Farm

Committed to Excellence in Poultry Farming

Welcome to Ods Makana Farm Services

At Ods Makana Farm Services, we pride ourselves on raising healthy, happy chickens in a natural and sustainable environment. Our farm has been a trusted source of premium poultry products for over a decade.

We use ethical farming practices, ensuring our birds have access to open spaces, nutritious organic feed, and the best care possible. This commitment to quality results in superior eggs and meat that you can trust.
